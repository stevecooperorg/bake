-------
Ingredients:

75g softened unsalted butter.
100g light brown sugar.
50g golden syrup.
125ml double cream.
3g table salt.

Method:

Use the pan.
Add 75g softened unsalted butter.
Add 100g light brown sugar.
Add 50g golden syrup.
Heat everything.
Stir everything.
Removefromheat everything.
Add 125ml double cream.
Add 3g table salt.
Stir everything.
