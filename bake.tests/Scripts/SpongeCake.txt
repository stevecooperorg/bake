.bowl 250CaSu 250Butt cream [1Egg mix] repeat 5 250SRFl fold bake
-------
Ingredients:

250g castor sugar.
250g softened unsalted butter.
1 medium egg.
250g self raising flour.

Method:

Use the bowl.
Add 250g castor sugar.
Add 250g softened unsalted butter.
Cream everything.
Add 1 medium egg.
Mix everything.
(repeat 5 times)
Add 250g self raising flour.
Fold everything.
Bake everything.
