CARAMEL SAUCE

Ingredients:

125ml double cream.
100g light brown sugar.
75g softened unsalted butter.
50g golden syrup.
3g table salt.

Method:

1. Heat 75g softened unsalted butter, 100g light brown sugar, and 50g golden syrup in a pan.
2. Stir.
3. Remove from heat.
4. Stir in 125ml double cream and 3g table salt.

-------
SPONGE CAKE

Ingredients:

250g castor sugar.
250g self raising flour.
250g softened unsalted butter.
5 medium eggs.

Method:

1. Cream 250g castor sugar and 250g softened unsalted butter in a bowl.
2. Mix in 1 medium egg.
3. Repeat step 2 another 4 times.
4. Fold in 250g self raising flour.
5. Bake in an oven.
